HR Peripheral for Wear OS

Turn your Wear OS watch into a Bluetooth heart rate monitor for your fitness equipment.

Broadcast Your Heart Rate.

Your gym equipment, like treadmills and stationary bikes, can display your heart rate, but only if your watch can broadcast it. HR Peripheral solves this problem by turning your watch into a standard Bluetooth heart rate device that any compatible equipment can connect to.

  • Live heart rate broadcasting over Bluetooth LE.
  • Compatible with fitness equipment (treadmills, bikes, etc.).
  • Clean, battery-friendly interface with a live graph.
  • Runs as a foreground service to stay connected.
